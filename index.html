How do I Register for USAA Online and Mobile Access?


Welcome to your step-by-step guide for verifying your identity with USAA through the mobile app, using the secure portal at usaa.com/verify. Completing this process is essential for protecting your account and ensuring you can access all USAA services with confidence.

Why Identity Verification Matters

  • Prevents unauthorized access to your financial and personal information.
  • Protects your account from fraud and identity theft.
  • Ensures compliance with federal regulations for financial institutions.

Step-by-Step: Verifying Your Identity on the USAA Mobile App

  1. Download and Open the USAA Mobile App
    Ensure you have the latest version of the USAA mobile app installed on your iOS or Android device.
  2. Log In to Your Account
    Enter your USAA username and password to access your account.
  3. Access the Verification Section
    Navigate to the menu (usually represented by three lines or a profile icon).
    Tap on “Security” or “Account Settings.”
    Look for the option labeled “Verify Identity” or similar wording.
  4. Start the Verification Process
    Tap the verification prompt. You may be redirected to usaa.com/verify within the app or prompted to follow on-screen instructions.
  5. Provide Required Information
    Follow the instructions to enter personal details such as:
    • Your full name
    • Date of birth
    • Social Security Number (SSN)
    • USAA number (if requested)
    • Answer security questions if prompted.
  6. Complete Multifactor Authentication
    For added security, USAA may send a one-time code to your registered email or phone number.
    Enter the code in the app when prompted.
  7. Submit and Confirm
    Review your information for accuracy.
    Agree to any terms and conditions, then submit your verification request.
    You’ll receive a confirmation message once your identity is successfully verified.

Troubleshooting & Additional Support

  • Forgot Your Credentials?
    Use the “Forgot Username or Password” option on the login screen to recover your account.
  • Need Help?
    • Call USAA customer support for real-time assistance.
    • Use the online chat feature on the USAA website or app for quick questions.
    • If you’re unable to verify online, USAA may offer alternative methods, such as phone verification or in-person verification at a participating UPS Store.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I’m asked for additional documentation?
USAA may request documents like a government-issued photo ID. Follow the instructions in the app to upload or submit these securely.
How long does the process take?
Most verifications are completed within minutes, but some may require additional review.
Can I verify on desktop instead?
Yes, you can use usaa.com/verify on your web browser if you prefer.

Stay Secure

Verifying your identity is a vital step in protecting your USAA account. Always use official USAA channels and never share your credentials with anyone. If you encounter issues, reach out to USAA support for guidance.
